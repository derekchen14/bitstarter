Despite the deluge of information faced by today’s society, most people still lean on notebooks or whiteboards to record their thoughts or to sketch out a quick idea. These inventions are 130 and 50 years old respectively, yet we can’t seem to do any better.

Sure, companies have tried to make progress, but most tools are difficult to learn and cumbersome to use. There simply lacks an application that not only has the accessibility of a piece of paper, but also has the capability of modern software (e.g. undo/redo). Even more troublesome, the apps that are good at collecting ideas fail to let you do anything with them once they are in the system. You can't structure, organize or manipuate your own data. You can't view the notes in a format that matches the way your mind thinks. This is where Notable steps in.

Built on the simple idea that true productivity is about taking effective action, rather than just efficient action, Notable allows you to work with your ideas to facilitate the discovery of meaningful connections. More than just a tool for storing tasks or creating lists, Notable intelligently prioritizes those tasks to see which ones you should be taking action on first. Ultimately, by allowing you to actively work with your ideas, Notable helps you find the signal through the noise.

FAQ

How is this different from Evernote?
In addition to allowing you to easily collect information, Notable also allows you to easily structure and organize that information. Additionally, Notable is actually built on top of the Evernote platform, so all notes you create in Notable are also automatically synced into your Evernote account!
What are Notable's main features?
Notable has three main features. First off, the app provides a frictionless UI for organizing your thoughts and ideas. The goal was to make taking notes just as simple as writing on a piece of paper, but with the added flexibility of organizing them as well. Second, Notable has extension views tailored to specific subjects, such as Mindmap View, a Math View, or a (Flash) Card View for studying. Finally, Notable also brings you suggested content to supplement your learning. Our sophisticated algorithm offers you additional material you might want to consider to take your learning to the next level!
Notable says my changes are saved on my phone/ tablet/laptop, but my notes aren't showing up on my other devices. What's going on here?
Your notes are still being saved, but they are being stored locally, which means they won't be available to other devices if you are either of devices is not connected to the internet. (Learn more about working offline.) Please check that both devices are online and give the system at least 5 seconds to start syncing. There is also the very unlikely, but possible case, that either Notable or Evernote is undergoing scheduled maintenance. Please check the Notable Blog for updates about maintenance.
