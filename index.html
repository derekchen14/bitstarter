FAQ

How is this different from Evernote?
In addition to allowing you to easily collect information, Notable also allows you to easily structure and organize that information. Additionally, Notable is actually built on top of the Evernote platform, so all notes you create in Notable are also automatically synced into your Evernote account!
What are Notable's main features?
Notable has three main features. First off, the app provides a frictionless UI for organizing your thoughts and ideas. The goal was to make taking notes just as simple as writing on a piece of paper, but with the added flexibility of organizing them as well. Second, Notable has extension views tailored to specific subjects, such as Mindmap View, a Math View, or a (Flash) Card View for studying. Finally, Notable also brings you suggested content to supplement your learning. Our sophisticated algorithm offers you additional material you might want to consider to take your learning to the next level!
